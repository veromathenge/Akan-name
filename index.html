Akan Name Generator

The Akans are a meta-ethnicity who primarily live in Ghana and Ivory Coast. They have a fairly unique naming system, which is based on the day in the week a child is born, and on the gender of the child. So a boy born on a Monday would be named Kwadwo or Koho, and a girl would be called Adwoa. Each day has one or more variants, but there's usually not much to choose from.
